New York Pizza

  • Time: 10 to 15 Minutes
  • Serves: 3 to 4 people
Ingredients Quantity Pricing
Pizza Dough 14 Ounces $ 6.99
Tomato Sauce 5 ounces $3.00
Mozzarella Cheese 5-6 Ounces $1.50

Instructions :

Step 1: Stretch out the Pizza Dough so that it is nice and thin.

Step 2: Evenly spread the Tomato Sauce over the dough Make Sure that no spot has more tomato sauce than the other.

Step 3: Sprinkle the Mozzarella Cheese evenly around the Tomato Sauce-covered dough

Step 4: Put everything in the oven. Wait exactly five minutes and take the pizza out.

Step 5: Enjoy !!!!

Cookies and Cream Ice Cream

  • Time: 75 Minutes
  • Amount of People Served: 2 People
Ingredients Quantity Price
Heavy Cream Two Cups $3.67
Whole Milk One Cup $1.75
Sugar 2/3 Cups $0.85
Vanilla Extract One Teaspoon $2.00
Oreos 20 Cookies $5.50

Instructions:

Step 1: Combine cream, milk, sugar and vanilla in a saucepan and heat until the sugar is completely dissolved.

Step 2: Pour into a bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Chill in the refrigerator or in an ice bath until completely cool.

Step 3: Place the chocolate sandwich cookies into a gallon size plastic bag. Using a rolling pin or a rubber mallet, break up the cookies being sure to leave some larger pieces. Store the cookies in the freezer.

Step 4: Freeze in an ice cream maker according to the manufacturerâ€™s instructions. When the ice cream is nearly finished freezing, pour the frozen cookie pieces into the machine, and allow to mix thoroughly. (You will still have to stir it up a bit after the machine is done.)

Step 5: Store in an air tight container and freeze for an additional 2 hours.

Ramen Noodles

  • Time: 20 Minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity Price
Dried Noodle Block One Block $1.50
Seasoning Packet One Packet $0.50
Flavoring Packet One Packet $0.50
Boiling Water Two Cups Free

Instructions:

Step 1: Place the dry noodle block into a bowl

Step 2: Remove the seasoning and the flavoring from their packets and pour them simutaneously on the dried noodle block.

Step 3: Boil water for 10 minutes and when you are done, pour two cups of it on the dried noodle block.

Step 4: Mix the the noodles, seasoning, and flavoring together and cover it with a metal plate.

Step 5: To ensure that the noodles dont get soggy, you have to remove the metal plate three minutes after you have put it over the noodles.

Step 6: Stir and enjoy !!!

Frozen Yogurt

  • Time: 6 to 7 hours
  • Amount of People: 4
Ingredients Quantity Price
Frozen Strawberries 2 Cups $3.00
Honey 2 Tablespoons $1.50
Plain Greek Yogurt 1/4 Cups $1.00
Fresh Lemon Juice 1/2 Tablespoon $2.50

Instructions:

Step 1: Add the frozen strawberries, honey (or agave nectar), yogurt and lemon juice into a food processor (or liquidizer).

Step 2: Process until creamy, about 2-3 minutes. Scrape down the side of the bowl to make sure there are no lumps left.

Step 3: Transfer it to an airtight freezable container and freeze for a minimum 6 hours but preferably overnight. Store it in the freezer for up to 1 month.
