All About The King

- Posted 6/22/18

Hello, this is Lebron James. As you all know, I am argueably the best Basketball player in the world. I have won over 3 NBA championships, held the title of MVP in both finals and regular season over 8 times. I have also made over 14 all-star appeareances in my career in the NBA. Over my career, i have been the the Miami Heat and my current team the Cleveland Cavaliers. I have won numerous championships in both teams, but we have reached a stump in my career as a cavalier when facing the Golden State Warriors.
